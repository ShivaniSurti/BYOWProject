Description

These are a beautiful species wildly known for their vibrant colors, feathers, and majestic appearance. There are many stories and folklure about these mystical creatures, let's take sometime to learn more about them!

Species of Peacocks

  • Indian Peafowl
  • Congo Peafowl
  • Green Peafowl

Places where peacocks are found include:

  • Sri Lanka
  • Myanmar
  • Java
  • India
  • Pakistan
  • Bhutan
  • Nepal
  • Australia
  • New Zealand
  • Flordia
  • Bahamas

Eating Habits

This species are ground eaters meaning that their source of diet comes from things avaliable from the ground. They are omnivores meaning that they eat both plants and animals. Their diet consists of plants, seeds, termites, ants, locust and even small reptiles.

Endangered or Safe?

Overhunting has caused these beautiful species to become in a vulnerable state which could lead to extinction according to the IUCN

Fun Facts

  • Female peacocks are called peahens and the both genders together are called peafowl.
  • Young peacock children are called peachick
